Traffic Cams provides access to over 13,000 traffic cameras in the United States and Canada via the TomTom API.

Use the map view or the Search charm to find the cameras within a given area, then select a camera to see the latest image available. As the selected camera refreshes, the next photo in sequence will automatically appear, and you can tap the camera icon at the bottom right of the image to play through the series of photos.

Via the app bar you can overlay traffic and switch between and aerial and road modes.
